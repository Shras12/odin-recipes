Chipotle Barbacoa

Barbacoa Bowls

Chipotle seasoned beef made in a slow cooker or dutch oven

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chuck roast
  • 2 Tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 3 tsp adobo seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp chili powder

Sauce

  • handful of cilantro
  • 1/4 can chipotle chilis in adobo
  • 3 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2-4 cups beef broth
  • juice from 1 lime

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 if using the a dutch oven.
  2. Sear meat working in batches until they are each browned and cripsy. Place in slow cooker or dutch oven.
  3. In a blender, blend the sauce. Pour over the meat.
  4. Cook in slow cooker for 8 hours, or oven for 3.
  5. Shred meat and use in bowls with rice, beans, avocado cilantro line ranch, tomatoes and romaine lettuce.
