Roasted Red Pepper Pasta

A quick sauce, best served with pasta and grilled chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp avocado oil
  • 3-4 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/4 c water
  • half a can of coconut milk
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • onion and garlic powder
  • kosher salt and pepper
  • red chili flakes
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Heat a stainless steel skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the oil and tomato paste and cook about 10 minutes stirring often. Use water a little at a time to thin and help with sticking.
  3. Roast peppers in a 420 oven for 25 minutes. Blend those with coconut milk. Add sauce to the pan and stir.
  4. Lower the heat and simmer, add the spices and cheese.
  5. Add more salt if needed, thin with more water or milk as needed.
  6. Serve over cooked noodles and grilled chicken.
