Easy Skillet Lasagna

A classic comfort food made in one pan for a quick meal

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb italian sausage
  • half an onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt and pepper

Cheese Filling

  • 1 15oz cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • cooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • italian parsley for garnish, opt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. In a 12 inch cast iron skillet, heat oil over medium heat, add sausage and ground beef. Add onion, garlic, seasoning and tomato paste.
  3. Add marinara and 1/4 c water. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add all the cheese mixture into a bowl and mix well.
  5. Layer ingredients starting with some meat sauce, then noodles finishing with cheese and meat sauce.
  6. Top with mozzarella and cover with foil.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes.
